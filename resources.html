Intersectionality 🤝
Intersectionality is mutiple ways of expression in this complex world. These can be gender, race, and relgion, but not them alone however its how they are together and have an influence on one another shaping our overal self. It is to show the way on how multiple different forms of inequality are at a dissadvantage. And with these dissadvantages comes barriers that other people may not understand.

The intersectionality wars an article by VOX goes in depth about intersectionality and how those feared it and covers Ben Shaprio who is controversial conservative political commentator and was one of the readings about intersectionality was written by and where he talks about intersectionality as a "totem poll" depending on who you are more on the outside will determine where you are on it.You can access his reading on intersectionality by going to What Is Intersectionality - Ben Shapiro
Implicit bias
Implicit bias is how we see others and associate them with things. This can also be thought of as stereotypes people have developed about certain influences. We took an implicit bias test through Harvard. There are multiple different tests on their site, you can pick anyone you'd like to take. It will test you in different areas to see your insight on topics. If you would like to take one you can click this link to begin.
